# Intellectual Property (IP) Considerations

## Intellectual Property (IP) Considerations

This section clarifies ownership rights within the EdEHR ecosystem to ensure transparency and protect the interests of all stakeholders.

### Platform Ownership

#### EdEHR Platform and Source Code

All intellectual property related to the EdEHR platform—including source code, system architecture, core functionality, and technical infrastructure—is owned by the EdEHR development team and contributors. This includes:

* Core platform code and architecture
* System features and functionality
* Technical improvements and enhancements
* Bug fixes and security updates

The platform is distributed under the AGPL-3.0 open source license, which governs how the code can be used, modified, and redistributed while ensuring the platform remains open and accessible to the community.

### Content Ownership

#### Educational Materials and User Content

Content created by users within the EdEHR platform remains the intellectual property of the content creator or their institution. This includes:

* Patient cases and clinical scenarios
* Curriculum materials and learning objectives
* Assessment tools and assignments
* Documentation and educational resources
* Any other materials uploaded or created within the platform

#### Key Principles

* **Retained ownership:** Your educational content remains your property
* **No automatic licensing:** EdEHR does not claim rights to your materials
* **Controlled sharing:** You decide if and how to share your content with others
* **Privacy protection:** Private content remains private unless you choose otherwise

### Contributions and Licensing

#### Code Contributions

When contributing code improvements or platform enhancements to EdEHR, contributions are governed by the AGPL-3.0 license. This ensures that improvements benefit the entire community while maintaining the open source nature of the project.

#### Content Sharing

If you choose to share educational materials publicly through EdEHR, please ensure you have the necessary rights to do so and clearly specify any usage restrictions or licensing requirements.

### Trademarks and Branding

The EdEHR name, logo, and associated branding elements are proprietary to EdEHR. Use of these assets requires explicit written permission and should not imply endorsement or official partnership without authorization.

### Contact and Support

For questions about intellectual property rights, contribution guidelines, or content licensing, please contact the EdEHR team directly. We're here to help clarify any concerns and ensure your rights are protected.

### Summary

* **Platform:** EdEHR owns the technical platform and code (AGPL-3.0 licensed)
* **Content:** Users retain ownership of their educational materials
* **Contributions:** Code contributions are shared under AGPL-3.0
* **Branding:** EdEHR trademarks require permission for use
* **Control:** You decide what to share and what to keep private
