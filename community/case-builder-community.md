# Case Builder Community

### Case Library

Browse our community case library for:

* Nursing scenarios
* Laboratory science exercises
* Interprofessional activities
* Specialty-specific simulations

### Contributing Cases

#### Submission Guidelines

1. Ensure your case meets our educational standards
2. Format according to our case template
3. Include clear learning objectives
4. Submit through the case portal

#### Quality Standards

All community cases undergo peer review evaluating:

* Educational value
* Clinical accuracy
* Technical implementation
* Accessibility and usability

### Collaborative Development

#### Finding Collaborators

* Post in #case-collaboration on Slack
* Join a discipline-specific working group
* Attend a case-building workshop
* Partner with colleagues at your institution

#### Interprofessional Cases

We especially encourage cases that span multiple healthcare disciplines. Learn how to develop effective interprofessional cases.
