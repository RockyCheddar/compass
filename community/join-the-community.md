# Join the Community

## Join Our Community

Connect with other EdEHR users and contributors through our various communication channels.

### Slack Channel

Our community Slack workspace is the central hub for discussions, support, and collaboration.

#### How to Join

1. Request an invitation by filling out this form
2. Check your email for the invitation link
3. Create your account and introduce yourself in #introductions

#### Key Channels

* **#general**: Community-wide announcements and discussions
* **#technical-help**: Get assistance with technical issues
* **#case-sharing**: Share and discover educational cases
* **#feature-requests**: Suggest and discuss new features
* **#implementation**: Discuss institutional implementation strategies

### Discussion Forums

For more structured, searchable discussions, visit our community forums.

### Events Calendar

Stay informed about upcoming events:

* **Webinars**: Monthly educational sessions
* **Training Workshops**: Hands-on learning opportunities
* **User Conferences**: Annual in-person gatherings
* **Office Hours**: Drop-in sessions with the core team

Subscribe to our calendar to add events to your personal schedule.

### Community Guidelines

To ensure a positive community experience for everyone:

1. Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions
2. Focus on constructive feedback
3. Share knowledge generously
4. Respect privacy and confidentiality
5. Follow channel-specific guidelines

Read our full Code of Conduct
