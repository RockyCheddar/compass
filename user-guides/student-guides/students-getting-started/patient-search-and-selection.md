# Patient Search and Selection

## Patient Chart Workflow

This page walks you through a typical “charting session” from opening the patient to saving a verified report and getting ready to submit your work.

***

### Locate the correct patient

1. Click **Search** in the top-left of the chart.
2. Type part of the family name **or** MRN—partial matches work.
3. Choose the patient; edEHR opens them in a new tab.
4. Repeat to view multiple patients; click any tab to switch views
