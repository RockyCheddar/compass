### What is EdEHR?

* EdEHR is a **simulated electronic health record (EHR) and lab information system** for learning.
* It‚Äôs designed for education ‚Äî simple enough to support your practice without the complexity of a real hospital system.
* The focus is on helping you practice **documentation, communication, and clinical reasoning skills** in a digital environment.

## Launch EdEHR from your LMS

All student access to **EdEHR** begins inside your institution‚Äôs learning-management system (LMS).\
Follow the three quick steps below and you‚Äôll be charting in seconds.

1. **Log in** to your LMS as usual and open the appropriate course.
2. Locate the **EdEHR activity link** provided by your instructor.
3. Click the link  ‚Üí  EdEHR opens in a new browser tab with the correct role, course and patient already set.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Tip:** Bookmark your LMS page. If you close EdEHR accidentally, simply re-open the activity link in your LMS.
{% endhint %}

### How to Access EdEHR

* You access EdEHR **only through your school‚Äôs Learning Management System (LMS)**.
* Your instructor will create course activities that link directly to EdEHR.
* **There is no separate login page.** Clicking the activity in your LMS takes you straight into EdEHR.
* EdEHR only records your first and last name so your instructor can identify your work. No other personal data is collected.

### Tips

* After you submit an assignment, the dashboard will update to show your status and any feedback from your instructor.

* The ‚Äúexit to LMS‚Äù link takes you back to your main course page in your school‚Äôs LMS. If you do not see this link it is because your LMS does not support this feature.&#x20;
